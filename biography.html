Biography

I have three college degrees, two Associate's and one Bachelor's. I graduated from Sussex County Community College (SCCC) with a degree in Business Administration in 2014. I went on to get a dual degree in Engineering Science and Mathematics from SCCC in 2016. I then went to New Jersey Institute of Technology where I graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in Information Technology with the goal of getting into the field of CyberSecurity. After graduation I was recruited by Revature LLC and proceeded to join that company in January of 2019. I was trained in Java full stack developement with emphasis on automation testing and was assigned a client project at Accenture Federal Services where I was until May 2020.
