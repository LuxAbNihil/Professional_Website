Hobbies and Interests

Web Development
My primary professional interest is Full Stack web development. I love building new applications and learning new technologies and how to use existing technologies in new ways. I want to become an expert in Java and Angular but I am open to learning any web development technology!
Nature Photogaraphy
I greatly enjoy taking pictures of all sorts of natural things! Some of my favorite subjects are reptiles, amphibians, and mushrooms. I also like taking pictures of rainbows and my favorite weather phenomenon are thunderstorms.
Space Exploration
Ever since I was a kid I have had an interest in space exploration. I eagerly await the moment when humanity establishes its first permanent presence on another planet! I am also excited by the prospect of a permanent moon base that can serve as a scientific outpost and experiment for developing extraterrestial human habitats!
Minerals and Fossils
For many years I have had an iterest in geology and particularly in minerals and fossils. I have spent many years collecting different specimens and have found some very interesting items over the years.
Creative Writing
I have always loved to read and from my passion for the written word of others, I developed an interest in writing my own works. I like to write poetry (its bad, but I do it anyway), and sometimes write short stories. I particulary enjoy fantasy, as it is the genre I was raised on.
